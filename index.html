A Profitable Product Launch Doesn't Just Happen.

Learn how to launch into revenue from day one.

We've brought together our stories as well as interviews with dozens of founders from every step of the journey. We seriously can't wait to share this book with you.



Products don't just magically make money. There's tons of groundwork that goes into any successful launch. In this book you'll learn our collective techniques for:

1

Building a quality audience

Generate excitement and validation long before your product is ready. Don't wait until the end and launch to crickets.

2

Talking to your peers

Strengthen your ideas and approaches by talking to folks who have already been in your shoes. Ask the right questions.

3

Developing the right product

Hone in to the real pain so you build what your customers actually need. Listen to the problem, not the people.

4

Generating revenue before launch

Master the art of turning your beta testers into paying customers. Change the conversations you're having.

5

Launching Your Product

Follow a proven strategy to take your product to market with no advertising budget. Embrace organic sources.

6

Optimizing Your Funnel

Learn how to develop and optimize your sales funnel as your traffic increases. Move people from A-to-ARRR.
